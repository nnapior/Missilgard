Let's Play

About the Game

  • Controls

    Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move around and avoid incoming bullets. You can also use the SHIFT key for fine tuned control and slow down your ship.

  • Multiple Bosses

    There is a variety of bosses to select from or you can choose to fight them all. Each one has unique bullet types, patterns, and new challenges to face.
