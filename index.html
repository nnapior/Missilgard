Missilgard

By Travis Bahadur, Yen-An Chen, Nick Napior, Trevor Seidel, and Prescott Weems.

About the Game

  • Controls

    Use WASD keys to move around and avoid incoming bullets. You can also use the shift key for fine tuned control and slow down your ship.

  • Multiple Bosses

    There is a variety of bosses to select from or you can choose to fight them all. Each one has unique bullet types, patterns, and new challenges to face.

  • Unique Bullet Paterns

    Each boss uses a variety of bullet types and patterns.
